What is Nutrition?

Nutrition is the process of eating the right foods that your body needs to stay healthy, grow, and have energy. It includes nutrients like carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water, which help your body function properly. A balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, grains, and proteins keeps your body strong, improves immunity, and prevents diseases.
